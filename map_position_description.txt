I used the Cincinnati, Ohio data from MapZen, available for download here:
https://s3.amazonaws.com/metro-extracts.mapzen.com/cincinnati_ohio.osm.bz2. You
can also find it by searching for Cincinnati on this page:
https://mapzen.com/metro-extracts/

The Cincinnati area is near the southwestern corner of Ohio, near the Kentucky
border. I chose the Cincinnati data because I grew up in Cincinnati.
